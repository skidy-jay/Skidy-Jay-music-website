"Overseas" is Skidy Jay's boldest release yet - a sensual Afro-fusion anthem that turns desire into rhythm. The song's core line, "Ama take you overseas" isn't about travel; it's about taking a woman to the ultimate level of pleasure and connection.

Blending Skidy Jay's smooth delivery with addictive beats and Kelvinet blended the second verse with Phyno kind of vibe, the track captures that wild energy of passion, confidence, and control. "Overseas" is made for the late-night vibe - the kind of song turns up the heat and keeps the room moving long after the last note fades.
