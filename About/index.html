Biography

Alexander Dominic , professionally known as Skidy Jay , is a Nigeria recording artist and creative technologist born on May 15, 1997 , in Lagos, with roots in Benue state. Growing up in the bustling heart of Lagos exposed him to a rich fusion of afrobeat, R&B and street rhythms that continue to shape his sound. His music is characterized by raw emotion, storytelling, and a distinct Lagos energy that connects deeply with both local and international audiences.

Since his debut, Skidy Jay has built an impresssive catalog that showcases his versatility and evolving artistry. His discography includes :
